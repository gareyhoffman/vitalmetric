Privacy Policy

Short version: we don't collect anything.

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

TL;DR

✅ No accounts. You don't sign up, log in, or create a profile.

✅ No data collection. Your height, weight, measurements, and calculator results never leave your browser. We don't have a backend server to send them to.

✅ No cookies. We don't set tracking cookies. The only thing stored in your browser is your fasting streak (in localStorage), and it never leaves your device.

✅ No analytics. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking.

What We Collect

Nothing. All calculations happen entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your inputs — age, gender, weight, measurements — are processed locally and never transmitted anywhere. There is no server, no database, and no API endpoint to receive your data.

What About localStorage?

The Fasting Tracker uses your browser's localStorage to remember your fasting streak across visits. This data stays on your device and is never sent to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

Hosting

VitalMetric is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data (like page view counts) as part of their standard CDN service. This is not personally identifiable and is not shared with third parties beyond Cloudflare's infrastructure.

Third-Party Services

None. We don't embed third-party scripts, analytics, ads, trackers, or social media widgets.

Children's Privacy

VitalMetric does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any information at all, this is trivially satisfied.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (unlikely given our architecture), we'll update the date at the top of this page. If we ever add analytics or data collection, we'll make it obvious and opt-in.
