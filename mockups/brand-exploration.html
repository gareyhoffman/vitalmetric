Recommended Brand Upgrades

Ranked by impact. Start with #1 — it's a 10-minute CSS change.

1

Bigger Logo + Tagline

Bump logo from 28px to 48-56px. Add "Know Your Numbers" tagline under the wordmark in coral. Instantly makes the brand feel established.

2

Subtle Dot Pattern on All Heroes

Same small SVG dot pattern behind every hero gradient. Instant brand recognition regardless of which page someone lands on. 12 lines of CSS.

3

Hero Illustrations (1 per calculator)

Small SVG illustration in each hero related to the topic — a gauge, a body chart, a plate diagram, a water drop. Gives each page personality while staying on-brand.

4

Custom SVG Icons Replace Emojis

Replace 🧬⚖️🥩💧📉⏱️ with custom icons using the brand palette. Emojis look like a text message — icons look like a product.

5

Brand Motif — The Arc

The gauge arc from the logo becomes a recurring visual element — in section dividers, result highlights, and card accents. It's your Nike swoosh.

6

Favicon + Social Preview Images

Use the larger logo mark as favicon and OG image. Currently using a DNA emoji as favicon — invisible and unprofessional.

Implementation effort: #1 and #2 are pure CSS changes — 30 minutes total. #3 requires one SVG per page. #4 needs 6 SVGs. #5 and #6 are polish.
