The Fine Print

VitalMetric calculators provide estimates based on published formulas. They are not medical devices and should not replace professional medical advice. Body composition, metabolic rate, and nutritional needs vary significantly between individuals. Always consult a healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, exercise, or fasting routine.
