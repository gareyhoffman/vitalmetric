5:2 Protocol

Eat normally 5 days, restrict to 500–600 calories 2 non-consecutive days per week.

This protocol is not time-based — there's no daily fasting window. Plan your 2 low-calorie days (e.g., Tuesday & Friday) and eat normally the rest of the week.
